Concept Explanation

In the security industry, "quality" is measured by the relevance and accuracy of findings. A 500-page report filled with false positives is useless. A top-quality result provides a clear, prioritized roadmap to risk reduction.

At Securox IO, quality means comprehensive coverage. We don't just run a scanner and export the PDF. We manually verify every finding, explore complex attack chains that tools miss, and contextualize risks based on your specific business logic.

Our commitment is simple: No false positives, no vague recommendations, and no missed critical vulnerabilities.

Why It Matters

Poor quality testing gives you a false sense of security while leaving doors open for attackers. High-quality testing ensures:

  • Complete visibility into your true attack surface
  • Identification of "chained" exploits (combining minor bugs into a major breach)
  • Zero time wasted by your developers chasing false alarms
  • Clear executive summaries that justify security budget to leadership

How Securox Delivers

We maintain our high standards through a rigorous Quality Assurance (QA) process for every engagement:

  • Tier-1: Automated scanning with industry-leading commercial tools
  • Tier-2: Manual exploitation and verification by lead engineer
  • Tier-3: "Red Team" peer review to challenge findings
  • Tier-4: Final report audit by Delivery Manager

Common Questions

What does the report look like?

Our reports are divided into an Executive Summary (business impact) and a Technical Guide (steps to reproduce + code fixes).

Do you offer re-testing?

Yes. Every engagement includes one complimentary round of re-testing to verify that your patches effectively fixed the identified vulnerabilities.

How do you score risk?

We use the CVSS v3.1 industry standard scoring system, adjusted with environmental metrics specific to your business context.
