Who We Are

Securox IO is a specialized cybersecurity firm focused on penetration testing and security assessments. Founded by industry veterans with decades of combined experience, we have grown into a trusted partner for businesses seeking to protect their digital assets.

Our team consists of certified security professionals who are passionate about identifying vulnerabilities before malicious actors can exploit them. We combine cutting-edge tools with deep expertise to deliver comprehensive security solutions.
