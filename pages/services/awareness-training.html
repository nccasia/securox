Service Overview

90% of all cyberattacks begin with a human error. Your firewalls and antivirus software cannot stop an employee from clicking a convincing phishing link or reusing a password. Securox IO's Security Awareness Training transforms your workforce from your biggest risk into your first line of defense.

We move beyond "check-the-box" compliance training. Our engaging, bite-sized modules and realistic phishing simulations condition your employees to spot threats instinctually, creating a resilient security culture across your organization.

Our Curriculum

We cover the most critical threats facing modern businesses:

  • Phishing Lab: Identifying spoofed domains, urgent requests, and malicious attachments.
  • Password Hygiene: The dangers of reuse, sharing, and how to use Password Managers.
  • Social Engineering: Recognizing vishing (voice), smishing (SMS), and physical tailgating.
  • Remote Work: Securing home Wi-Fi and handling sensitive data in public spaces.

Methodology

01

Baseline

We send a blind phishing campaign to measure your current "click rate" vulnerability.

02

Education

Users receive role-specific training modules (e.g., Finance gets "CEO Fraud" training).

03

Simulation

We run monthly phishing tests with varying difficulty to reinforce learning.

04

Metrics

We track improvement over time, identifying high-risk users who need extra help.

We respect your time. Our micro-learning modules are 5-10 minutes long and can be completed on mobile devices.
